
              <p/><p>Given a list of non negative integers, arrange them such that they form the largest number.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>For example, given <code>[3, 30, 34, 5, 9]</code>, the largest formed number is <code>9534330</code>.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>Note: The result may be very large, so you need to return a string instead of an integer.</p>&#13;
