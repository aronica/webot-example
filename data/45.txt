
              <p/><p>&#13;
Given an array of non-negative integers, you are initially positioned at the first index of the array.&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
Each element in the array represents your maximum jump length at that position. &#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
Your goal is to reach the last index in the minimum number of jumps.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example:<br/>&#13;
Given array A = <code>[2,3,1,1,4]</code>&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
The minimum number of jumps to reach the last index is <code>2</code>. (Jump <code>1</code> step from index 0 to 1, then <code>3</code> steps to the last index.)&#13;
</p>
