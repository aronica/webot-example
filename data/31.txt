
              <p/><p>&#13;
Implement next permutation, which rearranges numbers into the lexicographically next greater permutation of numbers.&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
If such arrangement is not possible, it must rearrange it as the lowest possible order (ie, sorted in ascending order).&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
The replacement must be in-place, do not allocate extra memory.&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
Here are some examples. Inputs are in the left-hand column and its corresponding outputs are in the right-hand column.<br/>&#13;
<code>1,2,3</code> → <code>1,3,2</code><br/>&#13;
<code>3,2,1</code> → <code>1,2,3</code><br/>&#13;
<code>1,1,5</code> → <code>1,5,1</code><br/>&#13;
</p>
