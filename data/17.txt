
              <p/><p>Given a digit string, return all possible letter combinations that the number could represent.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
A mapping of digit to letters (just like on the telephone buttons) is given below.</p>&#13;
<p><img src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/Telephone-keypad2.svg/200px-Telephone-keypad2.svg.png"/></p>&#13;
&#13;
<pre>&#13;
<b>Input:</b>Digit string "23"&#13;
<b>Output:</b> ["ad", "ae", "af", "bd", "be", "bf", "cd", "ce", "cf"].&#13;
</pre>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
<b>Note:</b><br/>&#13;
Although the above answer is in lexicographical order, your answer could be in any order you want.&#13;
