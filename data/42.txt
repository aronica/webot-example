
              <p/><p>&#13;
Given <i>n</i> non-negative integers representing an elevation map where the width of each bar is 1, compute how much water it is able to trap after raining. &#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example, <br/>&#13;
Given <code>[0,1,0,2,1,0,1,3,2,1,2,1]</code>, return <code>6</code>.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
