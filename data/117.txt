
              <p/><p>Follow up for problem "<i>Populating Next Right Pointers in Each Node</i>".</p>&#13;
<p>What if the given tree could be any binary tree? Would your previous solution still work?</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
<b>Note:</b>&#13;
</p><ul><li>You may only use constant extra space.</li></ul>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example,<br/>&#13;
Given the following binary tree,<br/>&#13;
</p><pre>&#13;
         1&#13;
       /  \&#13;
      2    3&#13;
     / \    \&#13;
    4   5    7&#13;
</pre>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
After calling your function, the tree should look like:<br/>&#13;
</p><pre>&#13;
         1 -&gt; NULL&#13;
       /  \&#13;
      2 -&gt; 3 -&gt; NULL&#13;
     / \    \&#13;
    4-&gt; 5 -&gt; 7 -&gt; NULL&#13;
</pre>&#13;
