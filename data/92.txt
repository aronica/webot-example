
              <p/><p>&#13;
Reverse a linked list from position <i>m</i> to <i>n</i>. Do it in-place and in one-pass.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example:<br/>&#13;
Given <code>1-&gt;2-&gt;3-&gt;4-&gt;5-&gt;NULL</code>, <i>m</i> = 2 and <i>n</i> = 4,&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
return <code>1-&gt;4-&gt;3-&gt;2-&gt;5-&gt;NULL</code>.&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
<b>Note:</b><br/>&#13;
Given <i>m</i>, <i>n</i> satisfy the following condition:<br/>&#13;
1 ≤ <i>m</i> ≤ <i>n</i> ≤ length of list.&#13;
</p>
