
              <p/><p>Given a string containing just the characters <code>'('</code> and <code>')'</code>, find the length of the longest valid (well-formed) parentheses substring.&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
For <code>"(()"</code>, the longest valid parentheses substring is <code>"()"</code>, which has length = 2.&#13;
</p>&#13;
<p>&#13;
Another example is <code>")()())"</code>, where the longest valid parentheses substring is <code>"()()"</code>, which has length = 4.&#13;
</p>
