
              <p/><p>Implement an iterator over a binary search tree (BST). Your iterator will be initialized with the root node of a BST.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>Calling <code>next()</code> will return the next smallest number in the BST.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p><b>Note: </b><code>next()</code> and <code>hasNext()</code> should run in average O(1) time and uses O(<i>h</i>) memory, where <i>h</i> is the height of the tree. </p>&#13;
