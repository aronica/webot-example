
              <p/><p>Given a binary tree, return the <i>preorder</i> traversal of its nodes' values.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example:<br/>&#13;
Given binary tree <code>{1,#,2,3}</code>,<br/>&#13;
</p><pre>&#13;
   1&#13;
    \&#13;
     2&#13;
    /&#13;
   3&#13;
</pre>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
return <code>[1,2,3]</code>.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p><b>Note:</b> Recursive solution is trivial, could you do it iteratively?</p>
