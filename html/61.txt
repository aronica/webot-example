
              <p/><p>Given a list, rotate the list to the right by <i>k</i> places, where <i>k</i> is non-negative.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>For example:<br/>&#13;
Given <code>1-&gt;2-&gt;3-&gt;4-&gt;5-&gt;NULL</code> and <i>k</i> = <code>2</code>,<br/>&#13;
return <code>4-&gt;5-&gt;1-&gt;2-&gt;3-&gt;NULL</code>.</p>
