
              <p/><p>Given an array of size <i>n</i>, find the majority element. The majority element is the element that appears more than <code>⌊ n/2 ⌋</code> times.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>You may assume that the array is non-empty and the majority element always exist in the array.</p>&#13;
