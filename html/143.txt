
              <p/><p>&#13;
Given a singly linked list <i>L</i>: <i>L</i><sub>0</sub>→<i>L</i><sub>1</sub>→…→<i>L</i><sub><i>n</i>-1</sub>→<i>L</i><sub>n</sub>,<br/>&#13;
reorder it to: <i>L</i><sub>0</sub>→<i>L</i><sub><i>n</i></sub>→<i>L</i><sub>1</sub>→<i>L</i><sub><i>n</i>-1</sub>→<i>L</i><sub>2</sub>→<i>L</i><sub><i>n</i>-2</sub>→…&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>You must do this in-place without altering the nodes' values.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example,<br/>&#13;
Given <code>{1,2,3,4}</code>, reorder it to <code>{1,4,2,3}</code>.&#13;
</p>
