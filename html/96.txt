&#13;
<p>&#13;

Given <i>n</i> = 3, there are a total of 5 unique BST's.&#13;

    \       /     /      / \      \&#13;
     3     2     1      1   3      2&#13;
    /     /       \                 \&#13;
   2     1         2                 3&#13;
</pre>&#13;
