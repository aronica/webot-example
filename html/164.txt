
              <p/><p>Given an unsorted array, find the maximum difference between the successive elements in its sorted form.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>Try to solve it in linear time/space.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>Return 0 if the array contains less than 2 elements.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>You may assume all elements in the array are non-negative integers and fit in the 32-bit signed integer range.</p>&#13;
