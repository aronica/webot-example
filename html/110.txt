
              <p/><p>Given a binary tree, determine if it is height-balanced.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For this problem, a height-balanced binary tree is defined as a binary tree in which the depth of the two subtrees of <i>every</i> node never differ by more than 1.&#13;
