
              <p/><p>&#13;
Evaluate the value of an arithmetic expression in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reverse_Polish_notation">Reverse Polish Notation</a>.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
Valid operators are <code>+</code>, <code>-</code>, <code>*</code>, <code>/</code>. Each operand may be an integer or another expression.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
Some examples:<br/>&#13;
</p><pre>&#13;
  ["2", "1", "+", "3", "*"] -&gt; ((2 + 1) * 3) -&gt; 9&#13;
  ["4", "13", "5", "/", "+"] -&gt; (4 + (13 / 5)) -&gt; 6&#13;
</pre>&#13;
