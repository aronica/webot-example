
              <p/><p>&#13;
Given a binary tree and a sum, find all root-to-leaf paths where each path's sum equals the given sum.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
For example:<br/>&#13;
Given the below binary tree and <code>sum = 22</code>,&#13;
<pre>&#13;
              5&#13;
             / \&#13;
            4   8&#13;
           /   / \&#13;
          11  13  4&#13;
         /  \    / \&#13;
        7    2  5   1&#13;
</pre>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
return<br/>&#13;
</p><pre>&#13;
[&#13;
   [5,4,11,2],&#13;
   [5,8,4,5]&#13;
]&#13;
</pre>&#13;
