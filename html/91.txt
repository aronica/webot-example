
              <p/><p>&#13;
A message containing letters from <code>A-Z</code> is being encoded to numbers using the following mapping:&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<pre>&#13;
'A' -&gt; 1&#13;
'B' -&gt; 2&#13;
...&#13;
'Z' -&gt; 26&#13;
</pre>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
Given an encoded message containing digits, determine the total number of ways to decode it.&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
For example,<br/>&#13;
Given encoded message <code>"12"</code>,&#13;
it could be decoded as <code>"AB"</code> (1 2) or <code>"L"</code> (12).&#13;
</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>&#13;
The number of ways decoding <code>"12"</code> is 2.&#13;
</p>
